About Jacob

Skulls • science • molten glass

Glass blower currently stationed in Seattle, WA.

Jacob Weil is the artist behind WEILGLASS — known for heady, skull-heavy designs that look like they were pulled straight out of a neon toxic lab.

Every piece is hand-blown and one of one. Jacob blends technical function with color-shifting palettes, UV-active accents, and a heavy sci-fi / lab aesthetic.

To see the newest drops and in-progress shots straight from the torch, check out @weilglass on Instagram.
